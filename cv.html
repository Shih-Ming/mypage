Research Area

My research interest focuses on novel applications of microwave systems. I am currently developing spatially reconfigurable phased array formed by multiple unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs), which may be applicable to wireless power transfer (WPT) or wireless communications, supported by  Ministry of Science and Technology, Taiwan. My previous research topics include dual-band open-source-based wearable radar system, beam visualization system for phased array education, and ultra-wide band hybrid coupler.
